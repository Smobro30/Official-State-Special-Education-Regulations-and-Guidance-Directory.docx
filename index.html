When to Cite

In formal educational planning (IEP/504), legal proceedings, or administrative audits, you should cite the specific Administrative Code (Code) or State Education Agency document (PDF/DOCX) provided here to ensure compliance with the most recent state standards.

The "Official current source" represents the primary text governing the education of children with disabilities, comparable to federal IDEA Part B regulations but tailored to specific state-level definitions and timelines.
